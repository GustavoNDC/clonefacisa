Why study at UNIFACISA?

Studying at Unifacisa is a unique opportunity. We are an institution concerned not only with its theoretical training, but also with its transformative role in society and with its technical and practical skills.

We are honored to belong to the select group of the 5% best rated Higher Education Institutions in Brazil, according to MEC (Ministry of Education).

Scholarships & Discounts

RUDDER

The cheapest financing in Brazil with an interest rate of 0.0%*. The payment of the credit starts only 30 days after the completion or cancellation/closure of the course.
Learn more

CredIES

Student loan that allows students to pay 50% on tuition during enrollment and the other 50% upon completion of the course.
Learn more

Egress

Students who have already completed another degree at Unifacisa have their first enrollment free of charge and discounts on tuition throughout the course, including re-enrollment.
Learn more

Covenant

Students receive discounts on tuition fees according to the course of choice.
Learn more

Family

Students with first-degree relatives who wish to study at Unifacisa are guaranteed special discounts on tuition.
